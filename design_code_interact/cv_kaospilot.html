A full UX/UI design for KAOSPILOT toolbox

UX/UI and DESIGN SYSTEM on top of existing visual identity.

The toolbox is a separate website for KAOSPILOT with the goal to provide a quick reference to facilitation tools used while working with a group of students.

    GOALS

  • Audit of the main website and decide which elements of the visual identity are to stay.
  • Building the new visual design on top of existing identity (logo, some of the font-types).
  • UXR and persona development for the toolbox: who are facilitators and what do they need from this website?

    METHODOLOGY

  • The Double DIamond and User-cenetered Design with endless empathy.
  • Interaction Design, multiple stakeholder's interviews, user-research, visual design, iteration, testing, prototype.
  • Figma, HTML/CS/JS/React.js, GitHub, agile: Jira, Confluence.

This project is being built. Feel free to ask me how it's going.
